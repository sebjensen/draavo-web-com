COVID-19

Travelling During COVID-19

While travelling to film videos on public transport, I've been ensuring to stick to latest government guidance, including wearing a face covering, keeping my distance from other passengers / anyone I may be travelling with if they are not part of my household, and sanitising regularly. I also have not been travelling during lockdown, whether national or local. I've also tried to travel during off-peak times to limit contact with others. If any videos were filmed during lockdown, they were filmed as part of a different, essential journey, in compliance with government guidance.

If you have any enquiries, feel free to contact me on Discord, by joining my server here, or email me at business@draavo.com.
